## Why AI Helps

AI can help you research companies, understand roles deeper, practice answers, and get feedback faster.

## Before the Interview

Use AI to research the company, role, and interview style. Prepare concise stories for your strongest projects.

> Act as a recruiter. What are the key skills and qualities needed for a {role} at {company}?

## During the Interview

Use AI to calm nerves, structure your answers, and think through tough questions before the meeting starts.

## After the Interview

Draft a short follow-up message that references the conversation and reinforces your fit for the role.
