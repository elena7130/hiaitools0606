## Start with the Job Description

Paste the job description into your AI assistant and ask for the top skills, keywords, and proof points.

## Rewrite for Evidence

Convert vague responsibilities into measurable accomplishments. Keep every bullet tied to impact.

## Check for Gaps

Ask AI to compare your resume against the role and identify missing context, weak bullets, or unclear outcomes.
