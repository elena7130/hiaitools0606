## Use a Focused Brief

Give AI the role, company, your strongest achievements, and the tone you want.

## Avoid Generic Claims

Replace broad enthusiasm with concrete proof that connects your experience to the company problem.

## Edit the Final Voice

Use AI for structure, then make the final version sound like you.
